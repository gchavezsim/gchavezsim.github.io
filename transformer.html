Transformer Equivalent Circuit Calculation

An insightful journey into the automation of transformer equivalent circuit calculations using MATLAB and Simulink, facilitating accurate modeling and efficient problem-solving in electrical engineering.

This project showcases an automated MATLAB application designed to calculate the equivalent circuit components of a transformer from given ratings and test results, emphasizing the blend of programming and electrical engineering expertise.

The user-friendly interface prompts for transformer specifications and test data, allowing users to easily enter open and short circuit test results. The application facilitates the storage and retrieval of this data, making repeat analyses straightforward and efficient.

The MATLAB code dynamically generates a Simulink model, representing the transformer's equivalent circuits from both the low and high voltage perspectives, ensuring precise simulations for various electrical system analyses.

Through this initiative, I've streamlined complex transformer calculations, enhancing the precision and productivity of my academic and research endeavors in the field of electrical engineering.
